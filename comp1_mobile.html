Competition Overview

Round 1 - The Vision Forge

The competition begins online. Teams must conceptualize and submit an original mood board along with fashion sketches inspired by the theme. Creativity, storytelling, innovation, and execution will determine who advances. Only the most striking and well-developed concepts will survive this elimination round and secure a place in the finals.

Round 2 – Runway Reveal

Finalists will bring their vision to life on stage through a dynamic presentation of their designed looks. Participants must command the runway with confidence, styling precision, and impactful choreography that enhances the concept behind their creation. This round tests not only fashion sense, but stage presence and performance ability.

Round 3 – The Final Twist

In the ultimate challenge, participants will create a unique modification to their outfits, incorporating a show stopper. Teams must think creatively under pressure to elevate and transform their final presentation while maintaining cohesion, originality, and visual impact. This round will test adaptability, innovation, and true styling mastery.

Submissions will be judged on three pillars: Aesthetic Cohesion, Mechanical Innovation, and Emotional Resonance. We are looking for designs that feel both inevitable and impossible.
